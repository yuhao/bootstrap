About MobiTools



Computer architecture research has been largely driven by access and awareness of tools and other infrastructure. In this tutorial, we present a series of tools and other infrastructure that enables computer architecture researches in the mobile computing space. The tutorial will spans three key components of mobile computing: software, hardware, and end-users. The tools that we will cover strongly emphasize real system measurement, including performance, power/energy, user QoS experience.

Participate in MobiTools 2016

We invite participation primarily in two forms:


Present!


Do you have a mobile research tool you would like to introduce to a broader audience? Apply to present it at MobileTools! Mobile tools invites mobile research tool developers to give a thirty minute presentation about their tool and how to conduct productive research with it. An ideal presentation will introduce the tool and its underlying mechanisms and then walk the audience through a (possibly hands on) "quickstart" tutorial.


Apply to Present Your Tool

Attend!


Are you a computer system enthusiast and looking to get your hands dirty on some real mobile devices? Attend MobiTools! MobiTools will expose you to the cutting-edge mobile research and its infrastructures. Being co-located with ISCA provides an opportunity to have some great invited speakers and an exciting panel: a great opportunity to see the masterminds behind mobile computer architecture research.
